Our OEM Workflow

From concept to delivery, our streamlined process ensures retail-ready garments at high speed.

01. Design & Tech Pack

Submit your sketch, reference garment, or full Tech Pack. Our design team assists in refining blueprints and specs.

02. Fast Sampling

We select the correct fabrics and produce your custom samples within 7-10 days, allowing you to test fit and texture.

03. Production

State-of-the-art sewing machines and strict inline quality control guarantee that bulk orders match the approved sample.

04. Global Delivery

We package to your retail specifications and ship globally using fast air freight or reliable sea logistics.

Production Craftsmanship

Discover the technical details and advanced processes behind our premium apparel.

Tech Pack & Patterning

Digitize sketches into blueprints and perform 3D fit testing for sizing consistency.

Fabric & Trim Sourcing

Source premium tech fabrics, dye custom Pantone colors, and procure high-quality trims.

Precision Laser Cutting

Utilize automated spreaders and laser cutters for clean, fray-free edges and ventilation.

Advanced Stitching

Apply flatlock (4-needle 6-thread) seams and bartacking for durable, friction-free performance.

Private Label & Packaging

Integrate heat-transfer wash tags, woven labels, hangtags, and eco-friendly polybags.

Multi-Stage Inspection

Perform inline check, manual trim cleaning, and professional steam pressing before packing.
